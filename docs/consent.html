Welcome to VibeAI FoldSpace

Emotionally Intelligent AI Chat Analysis

What VibeAI Does

VibeAI FoldSpace provides real-time emotional intelligence analysis for your AI chat conversations. It analyzes the tone, coherence, and sentiment of text as you type to provide visual feedback through our Resonance HUD.

Privacy & Data Handling

VibeAI processes all text analysis locally in your browser. We:

  • Do NOT send your text to external servers
  • Do NOT collect or store your conversations
  • Do NOT track your browsing activity
  • Only store your consent preference and basic settings locally

Beta Software Notice

VibeAI FoldSpace is currently in beta. While we strive for accuracy, the emotional analysis is experimental and should not be used for critical decisions. Use it as a creative tool for understanding conversational tone.

Your Consent

By clicking "Accept," you agree to allow VibeAI to analyze text in your browser and display the Resonance HUD. You can disable VibeAI at any time by removing the extension or clicking the privacy controls in the sidebar.
