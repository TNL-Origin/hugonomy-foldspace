QUICK START
-----------
1. Open Chrome and go to: chrome://extensions/
2. Enable "Developer mode" (toggle in top-right)
3. Click "Load unpacked"
4. Select the "dist" folder from this package
5. Visit ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or Copilot
6. Accept the consent modal to start

WHAT IS VIBEAI?
---------------
VibeAI is a browser extension that provides real-time emotional intelligence
analysis for your AI conversations. It helps you understand the resonance
and tone of your exchanges through visual feedback.

KEY FEATURES:
- Hugo Orb: Real-time emotional resonance visualization
- Prompt Coaching: Post-send guidance to improve your prompts
- Thread Analysis: Color-coded conversation flow
- 100% Local: All processing happens in your browser
- Zero Data Collection: We never see your conversations

PRIVACY
-------
VibeAI processes everything locally. We do NOT:
- Send your data to any server
- Collect analytics or telemetry
- Store your conversations
- Share anything with third parties

Click the extension icon in Chrome to view our full privacy policy.
