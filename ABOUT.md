## ðŸŒŒ What is VibeAI FoldSpace?

VibeAI FoldSpace is a Chrome extension that brings **emotional awareness** to your AI conversations. It analyzes the emotional tone of your chats in real-time and displays it through an elegant, non-intrusive HUD overlay.

Unlike traditional sentiment analysis tools, VibeAI uses the proprietary **HugoScore algorithm** to detect nuanced emotional states: Calm, Urgent, Reflective, Dissonant, and Resonant. These aren't just "positive" or "negative" - they represent the actual emotional texture of your conversation.

### Why "FoldSpace"?

The name comes from the concept of "folding" emotional space to make invisible dynamics visible. Just as Frank Herbert's *Dune* describes folding space to travel vast distances, VibeAI "folds" emotional space to make distant emotional patterns immediately perceivable.

## ðŸ§ª The HugoScore Algorithm

**HugoScore** is a proprietary emotional tone analysis algorithm developed by Joseph Tingling. It measures emotional coherence and intensity on a scale of 0-100.

### How It Works

1. **Message Analysis**: Extracts linguistic patterns, emotional markers, and conversational flow
2. **Tone Mapping**: Maps to one of 5 emotional states (Calm, Urgent, Reflective, Dissonant, Resonant)
3. **Intensity Calculation**: Determines the strength of the emotional state (0-100)
4. **Visualization**: Renders the result as a glowing emoji glyph in the HUD

Emotional Sovereignty is the idea that **individuals should have full ownership and awareness of the emotional dynamics in their AI interactions**.

This means:
- ðŸ”’ **Privacy**: Emotional data never leaves your device

## ðŸ”¬ The Technology

### Privacy-First Architecture

VibeAI FoldSpace is built on a **zero-trust privacy model**:

1. **No servers**: All processing happens in your browser
2. **No tracking**: Zero analytics, telemetry, or logging
3. **No accounts**: No sign-ups, no logins, no cloud sync
4. **Local storage only**: Uses `chrome.storage.local` for preferences
5. **Open source**: Full code inspection available

### Technical Stack

- **Chrome Extension Manifest v3** (latest standard)
- **Vanilla JavaScript** (no frameworks - lightweight and auditable)
- **Chrome Storage API** (local-only persistence)
- **HugoScore Algorithm** (proprietary emotional analysis)
- **Vite** (build system for optimization)

### Supported Platforms
