VibeAI Privacy Policy

100% Local Processing Zero Data Collection
Core Principle: Your conversations stay private. VibeAI processes everything locally on your device. We never send, store, or access your data on any server.

What VibeAI Does

VibeAI FoldSpace helps you stay mentally engaged while using AI — it detects thinking patterns in real-time (active, passive, or exploratory) and offers optional coaching nudges, focus controls, and reflection prompts. All analysis happens locally in your browser.

What Data We Access

  • AI chat text: Read-only access to conversations on ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini (current page only, never transmitted)
  • chrome.storage.local: Saves consent preference (consentGiven), bookmarks up to 100 entries (vibeai_bookmarks), session reflections up to 50 (vibeai_session_log), walkthrough version key (vibeai_intro_version), and HUD position — on your device only
  • localStorage: Saves session-level preferences including Focus Mode snooze timer (vibeai_snooze_until), walkthrough completion flag (vibeai_hasSeenIntro), HUD width (vibeai-hud-width), coach trend data (vibeai_trend_v1), and coach intensity level (vibeai_coach_level) — on your device only

What We DO NOT Do

  • No servers — we don't operate any backend infrastructure
  • No data collection — we don't log, store, or transmit your conversations
  • No tracking — no analytics, telemetry, or usage monitoring
  • No third parties — we don't share data with anyone (because we don't have any data to share)
  • No content rewriting — VibeAI only observes, never modifies your chat input or AI responses

Your Privacy Controls

Consent Required: VibeAI only activates after you explicitly grant permission. You can revoke consent at any time via the extension settings.

Focus Mode: Use the ••• menu to temporarily pause all nudges for 2 or 12 hours. Focus Mode is stored locally and expires automatically.

Coach Intensity: Choose Off, Minimal, Standard, or Active from the ••• menu. Your preference is saved locally.

To Disable: Remove the extension from Chrome, or use the ••• menu to turn off coaching entirely.

To Delete All Data: All data is local — uninstalling the extension removes everything. You can also clear chrome.storage.local and localStorage manually via Chrome DevTools.
